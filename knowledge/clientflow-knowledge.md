# ClientFlow — Company Knowledge Base

## Who We Are

ClientFlow is a growth automation agency that builds complete lead-generation and conversion systems for service-based businesses. We do not sell generic templates—we design, build, and connect websites, CRMs, voice automation, email follow-up, and workflow tools so leads are captured, qualified, nurtured, and booked.

## Core Services

### 1. Custom Websites (Conversion-Focused)

- We build modern, fast websites (including Next.js stacks) designed to convert traffic into booked discovery calls.
- Every site is structured around clear offers, trust signals, and strong CTAs—not just aesthetics.
- We set up tracking, forms, and integrations so leads flow directly into your CRM and automations.

### 2. GoHighLevel (GHL) CRM Setup & Optimization

- Full GoHighLevel implementation: pipelines, stages, tags, custom fields, and snapshots.
- Automated nurture campaigns, appointment booking, and two-way SMS workflows.
- Integration with websites, ads, forms, and voice/SMS tools.
- Team training and documentation so your staff can run the system daily.

### 3. HubSpot CRM Setup & Optimization

- HubSpot pipeline design, deal stages, properties, and lifecycle stages.
- Marketing automation, email sequences, and lead scoring where appropriate.
- Syncing HubSpot with websites, forms, and external tools via native integrations or n8n/Make.
- Best for teams already on HubSpot or scaling B2B/service sales with structured reporting.

### 4. Inbound Call Automation (AI Voice Agents)

- AI-powered receptionists that answer inbound calls 24/7.
- Qualify callers, answer FAQs, collect contact details, and book appointments.
- Log every conversation and outcome into GoHighLevel or HubSpot automatically.
- Ideal for dental, home services, real estate, med spas, and any high-call-volume business.

### 5. Outbound Call Automation

- Automated outbound calling campaigns for lead reactivation, appointment reminders, and follow-up.
- Scripted flows that feel natural and route hot prospects to your team instantly.
- Works alongside SMS and email sequences for multi-channel follow-up.

### 6. Email, SMS & Multi-Channel Follow-Up

- Automated follow-up email sequences (welcome, nurture, re-engagement, no-show recovery).
- SMS reminders and conversational follow-ups tied to CRM stages.
- Trigger-based messaging: form submit → instant reply → nurture → book call → confirmation → review request.

### 7. n8n & Make (Integromat) Automations

- We build custom workflows in **n8n** and **Make** to connect tools that do not talk to each other natively.
- Examples: new lead in Facebook Ads → CRM contact → Slack alert → assign rep → start voice/SMS sequence.
- Webhook handling, data transforms, error monitoring, and scalable automation architecture.
- Reduces manual copy-paste between apps and prevents leads from falling through cracks.

## Typical Client Problems We Solve

- Missed inbound calls and slow response times
- Leads sitting in forms with no follow-up
- Disconnected tools (website, CRM, ads, phone system)
- Manual outbound and follow-up eating team hours
- CRM pipelines that exist but are not automated
- Low website conversion despite ad spend

## How We Work (Process)

1. **Discover** — Audit current funnel, tools, response times, and bottlenecks.
2. **Design** — Map the ideal flow: capture → qualify → nurture → book → retain.
3. **Build** — Website, CRM, voice, email, and n8n/Make workflows implemented and tested.
4. **Launch** — Go live with monitoring, then optimize based on real conversion data.
